Profile

Let's learn about the man who makes our hearts go doki-doki.

Matsumoto Yukihiro, also known as "Matz", also known as Rubyのパパ (Ruby's dad), is a Japanese computer scientist and software programmer. He is best known for developing the Ruby programming language. However, he is also many other things, including:

  • a mormon,
  • a father,
  • and apparently a really nice guy.

He was also born in Osaka, where I lived for a year (so we're practically related). But, what don't we know about Matz? Well, lots. What's his favourite type of food? Does he prefer Morrissey or Matthew McConaughey? Final Fantasy 7 or Chrono Trigger? Some things we may never know.
